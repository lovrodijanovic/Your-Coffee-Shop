What we are about

Just like you, we are passionate about coffee. We love this hobby. It's more than just a beverage, it's a ritual. It's time we get to spend talking with a friend, partner, parent or grandparent. People of all ages and colours have their own unique coffee culture, and we would like you to be a part of ours.

Buying our product means supporting the pillar of this industry: farmers. We are working closely with Kenyan, Ethiopian and Brasilian farmers to ensure most of your money goes into their hands. We are working to cut down the number of middle-men your coffee goes through, ensuring freshest coffee with minimal expenses.

Choose a package that best fits your coffee needs. You can switch your subsription package at any time, with no extra charge. That gives you pleanty of room to experiment with our many coffee options.
